## Demo Call Process

```
BEFORE CALL (5 min)
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ • Review their info in HubSpot          │
│ • Note their property count/situation   │
│ • Have product open and ready           │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
              │
              ▼
INTRO (2 min)
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ "Thanks for taking the time. Before I   │
│  show you anything, tell me about your  │
│  current process for verifying income." │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
              │
              ▼
DISCOVERY (5 min)
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ • How many units?                       │
│ • How many applications/month?          │
│ • What's the biggest pain?              │
│ • What are you paying now?              │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
              │
              ▼
DEMO (5-7 min)
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ • Show the requester flow               │
│ • Show what applicant sees              │
│ • Show the income report                │
│ • Tie features to THEIR pain points     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
              │
              ▼
CLOSE (3 min)
┌─────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ "Based on what you told me, [plan]      │
│  makes the most sense. Want to get      │
│  started today?"                        │
└─────────────────────────────────────────┘
              │
         ┌────┴────┐
         ▼         ▼
    ┌───────┐ ┌───────────┐
    │  Yes  │ │ Objection │
    └───┬───┘ └─────┬─────┘
        │           │
        ▼           ▼
  ┌──────────┐ ┌──────────┐
  │ Sign up  │ │ Handle   │
  │ together │ │ & re-ask │
  └──────────┘ └──────────┘
```

## Sample Comments

**Answering a screening question:**
> "The pay stub problem is real. We've been using bank-connected verification for our properties - applicants link their bank and we see actual deposits. Cuts out the fake pay stub risk entirely. Happy to share more if you want to DM me."

**Responding to a complaint:**
> "Dealt with this exact issue last month. Applicant sent a PDF that looked off, turned out to be fake. Now we just verify directly through their bank - they connect it themselves, takes them 5 min. Way less hassle."

**Adding perspective:**
> "Good question. In my experience, the biggest time sink isn't the credit check - it's chasing income docs. That's where most applicants ghost."
