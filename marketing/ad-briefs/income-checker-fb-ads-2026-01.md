## Production Notes

### UGC Concepts (1, 4, 5)
- **Talent:** Property manager type—35-55, professional but approachable, could be male or female
- **Setting:** Home office or small business office (desk, laptop, some papers)
- **Props:** Pay stubs (printed), smartphone showing app, laptop
- **Wardrobe:** Business casual (polo, button-down)
- **Music:** Subtle background, upbeat but not distracting
- **Captions:** Required—white text with black outline, large font

### Screen Recording (Concept 2)
- **Capture:** High-res screen recording of Income Checker flow
- **Voiceover:** Confident, conversational male or female voice
- **Music:** Light corporate/tech background track
- **Annotations:** Highlight clicks, zoom on key features

### Animated (Concept 3)
- **Style:** Clean, modern motion graphics (think Slack or Stripe ads)
- **Colors:** Pull from Income Checker brand palette
- **Icons:** Simple, recognizable (clock, envelope, phone, checkmark)
- **Music:** Upbeat, modern, tech-forward
- **Duration:** Keep tight—20-30 sec max
